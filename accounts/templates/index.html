About Me

My name is Owen Cookman and I am a freelance web developer.
I grew up surrounded by a tech enthusiastic family. My father has always been interested in new technology and built our family computers. My eldest brother also gained an interest in this and became a computer technician later in life. My other brother studied computer science at university. During this time I also found an interest in coding and learnt a small amount from him but later followed my passion for music, although an amazing skill to have it hasn't led me to a career.
Seizing the oportunity to retrain in a skill that both interested me and was in high demand I studied how to be a software developer focusing on the process of designing and building websites from start to finish.

What I Do

As a Fullstack developer I handle both the front end and back end of the development process. The front end involves the design and overall look of your website, if you have a design and look in mind I will try to make it a reality. I can also provide consultation and/or suggestions to help make your website more user friendly and more appealing to your target customers/audience. The front end is everything that you can see and interact with on the web page while the back end is the turning cogs that you can't see that make the features of your website work. The back end involves things such as databases where user information can be stored or where all of the information about your products are held to be rendered on to a page for your customer to see.
Registering here will give you access to a contact page where you will be asked a few questions about your business and what you would like your website to do. I will contact you for any extra information that I might need and will keep you up to date throughout the development process. There will also be a status update in your personal profile.
