Many media agencies will build their customer's websites with as much reusable code as possible. Although this is an effective strategy to build websites quickly it can also lead to recognisable features where two different websites look very similar. I will build you a website with any design ideas that you have in mind and will try to keep the website as close to your business image as possible. If you don't have images and logos for your business I can put you in touch with a graphic designer if needed.

The use of appropriate colours and imagery will give your users an idea of your businesses identity. If you want users to see you as formal and professional, informal fonts and a mix of bright colours won't portray that.

I will look at your business and who your customers/clients are. Design with your target audience in mind to ensure that your business is portrayed the way that you want it so your users get the right idea about who you are.

The world of coding is constantly moving forwards.

To ensure that your website is future proofed I use and stay up to date with the latest coding languages. This will guarantee that your website is innovative and prepared for the ever changing world and utilises the cutting edge of web application features.

A responsive website has become essential to businesses as their customers and clients expect a smooth, easy to use experience no matter what screen size they are viewing their favourite website on.

Whether your users are viewing your website on a TV screen or mobile phone they should have a positive and easy to use experience.

I will ensure that your websitelooks great no matter what device it appears on. This will also help future proof your website as different screen resolutions appear on the market.

What I Do

As a Fullstack developer I handle both the front end and back end of the development process. The front end involves the design and overall look of your website, if you have a design and look in mind I will try to make it a reality. I can also provide consultation and/or suggestions to help make your website more user friendly and more appealing to your target customers/audience. The front end is everything that you can see and interact with on the web page while the back end is the turning cogs that you can't see that make the features of your website work. The back end involves things such as databases where user information can be stored or where all of the information about your products are held to be rendered on to a page for your customer to see.

Registering here will give you access to an order page where you will be asked a few questions about your business and what you would like your website to do. I will contact you for any extra information that I might need and will keep you up to date throughout the development process. There will also be a status update in your personal profile.
