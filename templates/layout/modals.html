Tailored Website

Many media agencies will build their customer's websites with as much reusable code as possible. Although this is an effective strategy to build websites quickly it can also lead to recognisable features where two different websites look very similar. I will build you a website with any design ideas that you have in mind and will try to keep the website as close to your business image as possible. If you don't have images and logos for your business I can put you in touch with a graphic designer if needed.

Targeted Design

The use of appropriate colours and imagery will give your users an idea of your businesses identity. If you want users to see you as formal and professional, informal fonts and lots of different bright colours won't portray that. I will look at your business and who your customers/clients are. Design around your target audience to ensure that your business is portrayed the way that you want it and your users get the right idea about who you are.

Modern Languages

The world of coding is constantly moving forwards. To ensure that your website is future proofed I use and stay up to date with the latest coding languages. This will guarantee that your website is innovative and prepared for the ever changing world.

Responsive

Whether your users are viewing your website on a TV screen or mobile phone they should have a positive and easy to use experience. I will ensure that your website looks great no matter what device it appears on.
